Alison Wonderland

I designed, implemented and ran visuals for artist Alison Wonderland on her US run from 2015 to 2016. For the first shows I created a custom Touchdesigner visual system that mixed together four Go Pro camera feeds and also designed all the original effects for the performance. Then for the NYE Countdown performance I designed all original generative visuals for the performance and everything was controlled by a OSC Jazzmutant Lemur.
