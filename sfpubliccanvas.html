For this project Madrone Studios teamed up with Bandaloop a group of aerial dancers to create a truly unique project. All the artwork was sourced from local content and then our team created animated sequences out of all the work. I built the custom server that ran the entire show for all 3 nights. I also was the person who created the entire playback system for both audio and music. The audio triggered all the movie clips seen and I had Albeton Live and Touchdesigner synced up. Truly a unique and inspiring project. It was nominated for a Isadora Duncan Award for Outstanding Achievement in Visual Design.
