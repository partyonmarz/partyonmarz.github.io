PartyOnMarz

PartyOnMarz - I Started this Before the World was Ending EP

PartyOnMarz is a project by Patrick Jagla a Portland, Oregon based music producer and songwriter. For years PartyOnMarz has worked as a visual artist and music producer combining the two styles for his daily sketches which are uploaded to social media. The unique blend of visuals and music ultimately has led to his first EP I Started this Before the World was Ending. The release is 11 tracks in total and showcases the unique instrumental and cinematic nature of the productions, combining elements of hip-hop, dub, lofi and boombap.
