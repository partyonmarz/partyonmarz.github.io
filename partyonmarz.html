PartyOnMarz

PartyOnMarz is a project by Portland, OR based artist Patrick Jagla.

PartyOnMarz - I Started this Before the World was Ending EP

The new EP by PartyOnMarz finds the visual artist and music producer showcasing their unique instrumental and cinematic production which combines elements of hip-hop, dub, lofi and boombap. The 11 track EP runs just under 21 minutes and is chock full of funky basslines, broken breaks and dubbed out synths.
